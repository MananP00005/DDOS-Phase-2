Our Story

Founded on a Vision of Excellence

Established in 1892 by a coalition of scholars and civic leaders, Westbrook University began as a small liberal arts college with 120 students and 8 faculty members. Over 130 years, it has grown into one of the nation's most respected research universities.

Today, our 400-acre campus is home to 42,000 students, 3,200 faculty and staff, 14 colleges, and world-class facilities spanning laboratories, arts centers and a 1.2 million-volume library.

Our alumni network of 280,000 graduates spans 150 countries, with notable members in government, science, industry and the arts.
